Pay with ATM

To pay at an ATM, please ensure you have the phone that receives your bank alerts with you in case you need to receive a token, then do the following:

1. Insert your ATM card and select the 'Quickteller' option

2. Select 'Pay Bills'

3. Select 'Others'

4. Enter '04225701' as the Biller Code

5. Enter the phone number being used with FastLoan for 'Customer Reference Number'

6. Confirm payment

7. You will see a confirmation message for your payment.

Kindly note that there is a N100 processing fee
